SOCIAL DEVELOPMENT PROGRAMME

SOLUSERV (GH) LIMITED has always participated in all activities organized by the Society of Petroleum Engineers in Ghana. SOLUSERV (GH) LIMITED is committed to the development of the society and will continue to contribute its quota whenever it is called upon to do so. We shall also develop a program for the development of any section of the society that we shall find ourselves related to. Social programmes for general developments of the Ghanaian society at large shall be part of the mandate of our corporate policy. Programmes for implementation of our social developments policy shall be reviewed on yearly basis.
