SERVICES

SOLUSERV (GH) LTD (SGL) is a locally owned 100% Ghanaian Company. We are registered with the Ghana Petroleum Commission to provide services to the Oil and Gas sector in Ghana.

DRILLING ENGINEERING AND DRILLING RELATED SERVICES

SOLUSERV (GH) LIMITED have two areas of operation. These are Drilling, Completions and Production Engineering Services and Drilling and Completions Equipment Sales and Rentals Services.

For the Drilling, Completions and Production Engineering Services, SOLUSERV (GH) LIMITED has the engineering capacity to support companies with Front End Engineering Design (FEED) and conceptual engineering work as well as Well Construction Operations and engineering support. In this regard, SOLUSERV (GH) LIMITED seeks to support operators with well drilling engineering and operational support where necessary. This will be in the form of clients’ in-house support as part of the engineering and operations teams either for short or long term. Secondly SOLUSERV (GH) LIMITED's engineering team is capable to support clients with engineering studies and analysis at more affordable rates and on short notice. For production optimisation, SOLUSERV (GH) LIMITED has the personnel to support clients’ operations effectively and cost efficiently.

For the Drilling & Completions Equipment Sales and Rentals Services, SOLUSERV (GH) LIMITED currently has representation agreements with the following companies:

Varel International Ltd for provision of drill bits, Geo-mechanical Studies and Drilling Optimization Support Services.

Downhole Products Limited for provision of Casing Centralization Products, Pilot Guide Float Shoes, Reamer Float Shoes and Tubular Cable Clamps.

TAM North Sea Ltd for Casing Inflatable Packers, FREECAP Swellable Packers, TRIPSaver Riser test Packers, Expandable Casing Packers, Casing Running and Filling tools and other similar Products.

OMEGA COMPLETION TECHNOLOGY LTD SOLUSERV (GH) LIMITED have an agreement with Omega Completions Technology Ltd for provision of comprehensive range of downhole products including highly advanced electronically controlled equipment like Pressure Cycle lug, Dual Equalizing Sub, Drift Junk catcher Hydrostatic Support Valve, Time Delay Equalizing Valve, Dual Cycle Equalizing Valve, etc. They are based in Aberdeen, Scotland. Please visit their website for further information.

TWMA for Integrated Drilling Waste Services including at the core of the company’s services the unique type of thermal desorption system (TCCRotoMill), capable of reducing drilling waste and drill cuttings to their separate component parts of base oil, water and solids and Cuttings transfer & Containment System (known in industry as Skip and Ship); Environmental Services and Technologies; Vacuum Tanks & Vacuum Cleaning Systems as well as Slops and Sludge Treatment; Decommissioning and Engineering Services.

WELL SERVICES, WELL INTEGRITY AND INTERVENTION AND PRODUCTION RELATED SERVICES

This is a range of services from Equipment Manufacturing and supply to Down Hole Intervention tools for logging in producing wells, and with product lines in the following areas:

  • Pressure Control Equipment
  • Slickline
  • Wireline / E-Line
  • Thru-Tubing
  • Subsea Support Services

It should be noted that the above services are provided to both International Operating Companies (IOC’s) as well as Services Companies working for the IOC’s.

SHOREBASE AND LOGISTICS MANAGEMENT SERVICES

WAREHOUSE & EQUIPMENT - SOLUSERV (GH) LIMITED, have acquired 4.31acres of land at Shama, 15 minutes drive from Takoradi, and has started work on the building of a Warehouse, Pipe yard, Office Facilities .

In the meantime, we have rented a warehouse at Sekondi/Takoradi which is under lease until we have our own facilities completed. We also have warehouse space in Accra, which we intend to use to service oil and gas activities in the eastern part of the country.

STAFF - Our current staff, include three very experienced engineers in the oil and gas sector having worked in Ghana and overseas. We have also recruited a young engineer this year that is currently under training as well as two technicians. We have one person currently under training as HSE coordinator.

We plan to increase the staff levels based on the level of activities from the services provided. All our current staff have undergone the DNV introduction course to ISO 9001, 14001 and OHSAS 18001. We have all undertaken the DNV internal auditing course and have attained the necessary certificates from DNV.
